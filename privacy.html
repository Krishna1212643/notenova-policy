Privacy Policy – NoteNova

NoteNova is an offline note-taking application designed to help users store notes securely on their device.

NoteNova does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

NoteNova does not use advertisements, analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or third-party services.

All notes are stored locally on your device. The developer does not have access to your data.

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Since the app works offline, your data remains on your device only.
